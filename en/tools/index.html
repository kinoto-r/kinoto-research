Japan Legal Amendment History Extraction Tool (Prototype)

This tool uses the e-Gov API to extract differences in legal provisions from amendment history and organize them visually in Excel format.

  • Purpose: Investigation of legal amendments and amendment history comparison
  • Output: CSV / Excel-oriented output
  • Functionality: Difference classification (addition, deletion, revision)

Regarding the scope of disclosure

The content displayed is limited to what is permitted to be made public.
Actual business data, client information, license-restricted documents, and confidential specifications are not included.
