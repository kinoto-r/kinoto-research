Japan Legal Amendment History Extraction Tool(Prototype)

This tool uses the e-Gov API to extract the differences in legal provisions from the history of legal amendments and visually organizes them in Excel format.

  • Purpose:Investigation of legal amendments, comparison of amendment history
  • Output:CSV Output
  • FunctionalityDifference classification (addition, deletion, revision)

Regarding the scope of disclosure

The content displayed is limited to what is permitted to be made public.
Actual business data, client information, license-restricted documents, and confidential specifications are not included.
